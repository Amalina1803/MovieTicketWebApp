CINEMA SOPS

Fully vaccinated audiences with Low Risk status are allowed entry into the
cinema regardless of age in Phase 4 states.

Only fully vaccinated audiences aged 18 and above are allowed into the cinema in other
states not listed below

Selangor. Kuala Lumpur. Putrajaya. Terengganu. Negeri Sembilan. Melaka

Social distancing is enforced with gap seats. Only members of the same
household bearing proof can sit together in Twin Seats

Masks must be worn at all times unless when eating or drinking

Foods and drinks are only available for purchase when buying tickets
online or at the e-Kiosk only
